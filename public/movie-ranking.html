The Best Movies According to Charu

My top 3 movies of all-time

Forrest Gump

My very first Tom Hanks movie. Loved the concept and acting and fell in love with Tom Hanks' acting there on.

Cast Away

Kept me on the edge of my seat. No one could have done justice to the role except Tom Hanks

Hera Pheri

Everytime i watch it, everytime i laugh so hard.. Great comedy of all times. Love the dialogue from Paresh Rawal = "Utha re le baba, utha le !! Mere ko nahi in logon ko utha le."
